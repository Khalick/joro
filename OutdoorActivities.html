About Jura Resort Outdoor Activities

Jura Resort is situated on the stunning Lake Side Beach. Our outdoor program blends adrenaline activities, calm nature experiences and evenings by the bonfire — perfectly curated for families, couples and adventurous guests.

Activities & Experiences

Water Sport
Kayaking
Paddle through serene waters and explore hidden coves around Lake Side Beach. Guided and solo options available.
Evening
Bonfire Night
Gather around a roaring bonfire on the beach. Live music, marshmallows, and stories under the stars — a Jura Resort tradition.
Cruise
Cruising Boat
Take a relaxing cruise on our comfortable boat. Sunset cruises, wildlife spotting and private charters make unforgettable memories.
Relax
Sand Bathing
Enjoy warm sand therapy and gentle lakeside sunbathing — a soothing, natural way to unwind at Jura Resort.
Water Sport
Swimming
Dive into crystal-clear water — choose between supervised beach zones and our resort pool for laps and leisure.
Nature
Nature Walks
Explore scenic trails with our expert guides. Birdwatching, flora tours and sunset walks are family-friendly activities at Jura Resort.

Highlights

Adventure of the Week — Kayaking

Explore secret coves and scenic spots with our guided kayak tours. Perfect for all skill levels.

Top Pick — Bonfire Night

An evening program with music, food and the warm glow of a beach bonfire — family-friendly and romantic.
