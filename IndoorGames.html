About Jura Resort Indoor Games

At Jura Resort, indoor fun never ends. Our game lounges are crafted to bring guests together — whether you want an energetic table tennis rally, a relaxed pool match, or a cozy board game afternoon.

Our curated selection includes table tennis, pool, board games, card games, darts and rotating seasonal challenges. Perfect for families, groups and solo practice sessions.
