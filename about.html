The Jura Resort Story: 15 Years of Lake Victoria Excellence

A journey of passion, tradition, and culinary excellence on the shores of Africa's largest freshwater lake in Migori County, Kenya

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About Jura Resort

Get answers to the most common questions about Lake Victoria's premier dining and resort experience

Where is Jura Resort located exactly?

Jura Resort is located on the pristine shores of Lake Victoria Beach in Migori County, Kenya. We're situated on Africa's largest freshwater lake, offering direct lake access and stunning panoramic views. Our exact address is Lake Victoria Beach, Migori, Kenya.

What types of cuisine does Jura Resort serve?

Jura Resort specializes in authentic Indian cuisine, traditional Luo cultural dishes, fresh Swahili specialties, and international fusion cuisine. We also feature fresh Lake Victoria fish preparations and offer comprehensive vegetarian and vegan options to accommodate all dietary preferences.

What activities are available at Jura Resort?

Jura Resort offers swimming in our heated pool, exciting water sports on Lake Victoria, scenic lake cruising, helipad access for luxury arrivals, live cultural entertainment, traditional music performances, and various indoor and outdoor activities. We're a complete lakeside adventure destination.

How can I make a reservation at Jura Resort?

You can make a reservation by calling us at +254722566089 or by sending a WhatsApp message to the same number. Our reservation team is available to help you book tables, plan events, or arrange accommodation. You can also email us at info@juraresort.com for detailed inquiries.

What are Jura Resort's operating hours?

Jura Resort is open daily from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM, offering all-day dining from sunrise breakfast to moonlit dinners. We serve breakfast, lunch, and dinner, with our comprehensive menu and expert culinary team ensuring exceptional meals throughout the day.
