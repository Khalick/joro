Nomad’s Restaurant

Offering quality local and international dishes, the Nomad Beach Bar and Restaurant is perfectly placed on Kenya’s Diani Beach. Providing an idyllic spot to relax between water sport sessions, enjoy sundowners with friends and savour a romantic, moonlight dinner, Nomad’s is the place to be on Diani Beach.

Our extensive a-la carte menu offers a fantastic choice of seafood, pizza and fine dining options, accompanied by an extensive wine selection and drinks menu. Younger diners will love our kids’ menu and delicious homemade ice-creams.

Available for private functions, the Nomad Beach Bar and Restaurant is also the ideal place to celebrate a special occasion or corporate event.

Fresh Local Ingredients

Nomad is committed to supporting local farmers and producers. Our extensive a la carte menu boasts an eclectic combination of local and international dishes made from the freshest local ingredients. Regular harvests from the Nomad organic farm deliver fresh produce straight to our kitchen.

Thinking Green

Nomad’s Executive Chef

Gabriele Raimondi

Executive Chef at The Sands at Nomad

Gabriele Raimondi, born in 1986, decided to follow his passion for food when he was 21. After training at culinary school and completing his apprenticeship at some of Rome’s top restaurants, he worked across Europe, experimenting, and enriching his knowledge of international cuisine and learning from other top chefs. Returning to Italy, he followed his passion for creating simple and sustainable cuisine made of high – quality products.

Since joining the team at Nomad he has continued to innovate and amaze with his creativity and wonderous flavours that run throughout all his dishes.
