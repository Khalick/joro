JURA RESTAURANT

Offering quality local and international dishes, the Juro Restaurant is perfectly placed on Kenya's Lake Side Beach. Providing an idyllic spot to relax between water sport sessions, enjoy sundowners with friends and savour a romantic, moonlight dinner, Juro Resort is the place to be on Lake Side Beach.

Our extensive à-la-carte menu offers a fantastic choice of seafood, pizza and fine dining options, accompanied by an extensive wine selection and drinks menu. Younger diners will love our kids' menu and delicious homemade ice-creams.

Available for private functions, the Jura Restaurant is also the ideal place to celebrate a special occasion or corporate event.
